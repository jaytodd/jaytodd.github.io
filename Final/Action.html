Action/Thriller Shows

The following TV shows are some of the best Action TV shows avaliable to watch. This is a large category that has significant representation from superhero, adventure, and war related shows.

#1 Band of Brothers

Band of brothers is a bleak show about a group of soldiers fighting in WWII. Tom Hanks and a very recognizeable cast portray a moving and realistic look into what it was like fighting in WWII. Being on par with his earlier work Saving Private Ryan, Stephen Speilberg is deserving of the number one spot for action TV shows.


#2 Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an extremely ambitious TV show that details the seprate but interconnected stories of more than 50 characters. The Game of Thrones world is very complex and extremely creative. This show always kept me guessing and excited for the next episode. Despite having its fair share of criticisms, Game of Thrones is worhty of the number two spot.


#3 24

24 is a long running show about crime that must be solved within 24 hours.


#4 The Punisher

Marvel's The Punisher is, in my opinion, the best super hero show that has come out. Most other Marvel shows that have come out felt too dragged out and lacking in action. The Punisher is superior to the other super hero shows because it shows a grittier and more captivating, action packed story.


Honorable Mention: The Boys

The Boys is one of my favorite TV shows of all time. The main reason for this is it fits in very well in todays TV climate considering how many super hero movies are being released. This show portrays a more grim and realistic version of what the world would look like with super heros.
